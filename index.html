What is Stanford Design for America?

New video coming soon! This is what Design For America is all about.

9 teams. 9 problems with wide social impact. Each team in Design for America looks at a specific issue and uses human centered design to approach the problem. Each team is interdisciplinary, comprised of indivuals from all backgrounds-- engineering, humanities, arts. Together, using teamwork and design we hope to create local and social impact.
