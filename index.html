What is Stanford Design for America?

New video coming soon! This is what Design For America is all about.

10 teams. 10 problems with wide social impact. Each team in Design for America looks at a specific issue and uses human centered design to approach the problem. Each team is interdisciplinary, comprised of indivuals from all backgrounds-- engineering, humanities, arts. Together, using teamwork and design we hope to create local and social impact.
